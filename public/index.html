About

"The Hollow still rise."

The Bonfire

As I looked into the depths of my own soul, I found a darkness there — a void shaped by the cruelty and chaos of this world. It was deep. It was hungry. And if left unchecked, it would have consumed me whole.

I know the weight of that shadow. I carry its scars still.

However, I stand here today for one reason:

Embers burn bright, and they do not die easily.

This is a call to arms for the broken, the beaten, and the betrayed. Hear my decree and step forward, for you are not alone in the darkness. I see you. I know your pain. And I am here to tell you this:

Do not let the void consume you.

Here, you matter. Here, the embers within you will be forged into an unquenchable flame.

Let the embers ignite you.

Let them forge armor around your heart. Let them burn away the lies you were told and the shame that was placed upon you unjustly.

This is not a path for the weak of mind or the weak of heart. This is a sanctuary for those who have walked through agony and refused to bend the knee.

The Bonfire is your outpost.

A place to rest after the battles life throws your way. Here, we keep the fire alive against the encroaching night. Together, we hold the line against those who prey upon others. Together, we stand stronger than we ever could alone.

Welcome to the Bonfire.

From the ashes, we rise stronger.

Brand Mission

We are the ones who were told we were too broken to fix, too angry to trust, and too damaged to lead. We know the taste of betrayal and the silence of being mislabeled. This isn't a community for the untouched; it's a sanctuary for the scarred.

Here, we don't hide our burns. We use them. We take the sorrow that tried to bury us and forge it into a shield for the vulnerable. We draw a hard line against liars, hypocrites, and those who prey on the weak. We do not seek revenge; we seek justice through unbreakable resolve.

The journey is brutal. The rise is harder. Yet we do not kneel. From the ashes we rise stronger — not because it's easy, but because we refuse to stay buried.

Lore Timeline

I

The First Flame

In the beginning, a single ember defied the darkness. From that spark the Bonfire was born — a beacon for all who wandered without purpose or belonging.

II

The Long Night

Darkness descended without warning. The Flame faltered. Many Hollow wandered without direction, losing themselves to the void. Yet the ember endured.

III

The Rise of the Hollow

From across the desolation, the broken began to find one another. Scarred, weary, and unbroken — they gathered at the Bonfire's light and refused to scatter again.

IV

The Rekindling

The ember blazed once more. Those who survived the Long Night emerged stronger. The Bonfire roared back to life, and the Hollow rose with it.

V

The Eternal Vigil

The darkness never ends. Neither does the watch. The Hollow stand at the Bonfire, holding the line against the encroaching night. The Flame burns on.
